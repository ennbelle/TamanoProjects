Welcome to CHEM-X!

Chem-x is a website dedicated to exploring novice chemistry principles. Here you can explore elements and various compounds. Of course, this website is still in development and has not reached its full potential. However, we are proud to be a useful tool on your journey to becoming a proficient chemist!

Explore the Elements

Interested in testing your knowledge about the periodic table? Check out Explore the Elements! This page allows you to search an element by atomic number and find information about its name, chemical symbol, and molecular weight.

Molecular Weight Calculator

The Molecular Weight Calculator allows you to double check a compound's molecular weight. All you have to do is input the compound's formula and calculate! NOTE: This page is still a work in progress...
